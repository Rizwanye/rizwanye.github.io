## Who I am

I am Rizwan. I work across business analysis and software delivery while
deepening my data science and cloud deployment skills.

This site is personal, but it has a clear theme: how useful technical work gets
from an idea into something people can actually use. That means understanding
the business context, asking better questions of the data, building maintainable
software, and learning how production systems are deployed and supported.

## Why this site exists

I use iRizwan to write about the overlap between business, data, software, and
production delivery. Some of the writing will be educational. Some of it will
be practical notes from learning, building, and working through technical
problems. The common thread is the same: useful work should not stop at a
document, dashboard, notebook, or prototype if it can become a reliable system.

## What I write about

The site focuses on a few connected topics:

- business analysis and requirements
- data science and practical forecasting
- software engineering for maintainable delivery
- cloud deployment with AWS
- open-source learning and public notes
- forward deployment engineering

## Forward deployment engineering

Forward deployment engineering is one way to describe the mix of skills I am
writing about. It is the label I use for work that starts with a business
problem, uses data honestly, and ends with software that can run in production.

That is why the topic matters here. It gives a name to the kind of work I want
to understand and explain, but it does not replace the personal side of the
site.

## What it means to me

I want the site to feel like a public notebook for learning, not a generic
marketing page. The personal layer matters because colleagues, friends, or
future collaborators should be able to see who I am first, then understand the
technical thread underneath.

## What I am building toward

The direction of this site is to become a personal record of learning, building,
and explaining how business-aware software gets made. The topics I expect to
write about here include:

- business analysis and requirements
- data science and practical forecasting
- software engineering for maintainable delivery
- cloud deployment with AWS
- open-source learning and public notes
- the forward deployment engineer skill set
