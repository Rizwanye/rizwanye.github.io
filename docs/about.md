# The Role

On this site, a forward deployment engineer is someone who can work across four
layers at once: the business problem, the data, the software, and the cloud
environment where the solution ends up running.

The point of the role is not to be broad for its own sake. The point is to
connect the people asking for an outcome with the technical path required to
deliver it.

## What this site is trying to say

This is a personal site about a role that sits in the middle of several
disciplines. It assumes the strongest version of the job is not a narrow title
but a delivery profile:

- understand the business
- understand the data
- understand the engineering path
- understand how to get the work into production

## What the role is not

- It is not only reporting.
- It is not only modelling.
- It is not only backend engineering.
- It is not only cloud administration.
- It is the combination of those pieces around a real business problem.
